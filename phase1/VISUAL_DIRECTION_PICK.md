## Locked aesthetic system

```text
Base:        #06101F  (navy void)
Fluid A:     #0E7490 → #2DD4BF  (cyan→teal smoke)
Fluid B:     #10B981 @ 20–35%   (emerald bloom)
Text:        #F8FAFC / #94A3B8
Accent line: 1px teal hairline
Motion:      fluid blob drift (bg) + SVG pop springs (fg) + stagger cascades
```

**Brand rule:** Fluid motion lives in the *background and icon layer*; foreground copy stays crisp and judge-readable. Trust > flash.

## What “like that template” means in Remotion
1. Full-bleed dark field (not flat — animated fluid mesh/blob).
2. Huge brand / one headline (S01).
3. Organic teal smoke/blob drifting slowly behind content.
4. Icons and cards **pop** with spring overshoot (SVG scale 0→1.08→1).
5. SVG stroke-draw for citation links / agent connectors.
6. Soft grain overlay (2–4% noise) for SaaS depth — not purple grain.
