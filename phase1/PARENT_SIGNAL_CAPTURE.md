## CRITICAL regulatory tailwind — SEBI AI accountability

- REs using AI/ML (in-house or third-party) are **solely responsible** for data privacy/security, **accuracy of AI outputs**, and legal compliance.

**Pitch gold:** RegOS is designed for a world where intermediaries are liable for AI outputs — hence mandatory human approval, clause citations, confidence thresholds, audit logs of AI actions, model card, and "not legal advice / decision-support" framing.
