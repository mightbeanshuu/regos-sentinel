> **Enterprise agentic compliance already exists** — OnFinance ComplianceOS for large BFSI, Corlytics/ClauseMatch for global Tier-1 banks. That proves the category.  
> **RegOS Sentinel is built for the segment those tools don’t serve in a TechSprint-visible way: SEBI’s lean stock brokers (Small / Mid-size REs)** who still translate the Stock Broker Master Circular, CSCRF, and SCORES↔ODR by hand.

## 5. Why-now bullets (5–8, sourced, pitch-ready)

1. **SEBI wrote PS2 for this exact gap.** TechSprint 2026 asks for agentic systems that turn unstructured SEBI text into machine-actionable, auditable compliance for intermediaries — RegOS is on-theme to the letter.  
   Source: https://www.globalfintechfest.com/gff-hackathons/sebi-techsprint · https://www.corplawupdates.in/updates/sebi-securities-market-techsprint-gff-2026

2. **Intermediaries are legally liable for AI outputs (Reg 16C).** Any compliance AI without citations, human gates, and audit logs is a regulatory liability — RegOS is designed for that world.  
   Source: https://taxguru.in/sebi/sebi-amends-regulations-ai-usage-intermediaries.html · https://sarthaklaw.com/sebi-update-securities-and-exchange-board-of-india-intermediaries-amendment-regulations-2025/

3. **SEBI’s chair put AI + Human-in-the-Loop on the 2026 agenda** and promised detailed responsible-AI guidelines — the timing for a trustworthy agentic compliance demo is now.  
   Source: https://www.etnownews.com/markets/sebi-to-issue-detailed-ai-guidelines-for-capital-markets-says-tuhin-kanta-pandey-at-et-now-markets-summit-2026-takeaways-from-sebi-chairmans-address-article-154582024

4. **Master circulars are being rewritten this month.** SEBI’s Jun 22, 2026 ease-of-doing-business review aims to cut exchange master-circular bulk ~50%; public comments run through **Jul 13, 2026** — RegOS rides the “text → operational action” wave while the rulebook is in motion.  
   Source: https://www.sebi.gov.in/media-and-notifications/press-releases/jun-2026/ease-of-doing-business-comprehensive-review-of-master-circular-for-stock-exchanges-and-clearing-corporations-and-the-master-circular-for-commodity-derivatives_102288.html

5. **The broker rulebook itself was reset in 2026.** SEBI (Stock Brokers) Regulations, 2026 replaced the 1992 framework and roughly halved the text while adding new institutional controls — lean brokers need a compiler + impact simulator, not more PDFs.  
   Source: https://www.sansalegal.com/post/sebi-stock-brokers-regulations-2026-key-changes-new-rules-and-compliance-guide-for-brokers · https://www.corplawupdates.in/updates/sebi-stock-brokers-regulations-2026

6. **CSCRF is an acute 2026 compliance event for REs.** Small/mid brokers face tiered cyber obligations (VAPT, board accountability, evidence) that map cleanly to RegOS’s evidence-graph demo.  
   Source: https://www.bachao.ai/blog/sebi-cscrf-compliance-stockbrokers-2026-complete-guide

7. **2025→2026 continuity:** GFF 2025 already stressed member compliance monitoring; 2026 upgrades that lane to **Agentic Compliance** — SEBI’s persistent need, now AI-native.  
   Source: https://www.outlookbusiness.com/markets/sebi-launches-securities-market-hackathon · approach-refinement.md synthesis · TechSprint PS2 page above

8. **Category capital is flowing — white-space is the underserved segment.** OnFinance’s $4.2M Peak XV round and Corlytics’ 2026 awards prove willingness to pay; RegOS’s wedge is the **small SEBI intermediary + sandbox-inspectable** slice those products don’t optimize for.  
   Source: https://inc42.com/buzz/bfsi-focussed-ai-startup-onfinance-raises-4-2-mn/ · https://www.corlytics.com/press-releases/corlytics-named-policy-management-solution-of-the-year-in-2026-fintech-breakthroughawards-program/
