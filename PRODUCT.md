# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

The primary audience is the SEBI TechSprint jury and regulatory-domain evaluators. They need to understand the product quickly, trust its controls, and see convincing evidence that RegOS Sentinel can turn regulatory text into operational compliance workflows.

## Product Purpose

RegOS Sentinel is a supervised agentic compliance operating layer for SEBI intermediaries. It converts circulars and other regulatory sources into clause-cited obligations, assigned tasks, evidence workflows, change-impact analysis, and audit-ready outputs. The presentation must make the system feel real, differentiated, and implementable rather than conceptual.

## Brand Personality

Watchful, precise, and kinetic. The experience should combine institutional credibility with the polish and confidence of a high-value SaaS product.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic static pitch-deck motion, random decorative jitter, unstable body text, consumer-app playfulness, purple AI gradients, excessive glass cards, neon overload, and abstract imagery that does not explain the product. Do not imitate editorial magazine layouts when a product interaction can carry the story more clearly.

## Design Principles

1. Show the product doing the work instead of describing features abstractly.
2. Use motion to reveal causality: source input, agent reasoning, human approval, operational output.
3. Keep compliance copy and evidence readable while concentrating kinetic energy in transitions and hero beats.
4. Make every visual flourish reinforce trust, supervision, traceability, or speed.
5. Give each scene one memorable signature action and let supporting motion remain restrained.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keep critical UI, citations, and compliance text stable and high contrast. Restrict jitter, rapid scale changes, and flashing contrast reversals to brief transition beats. Provide a reduced-motion rendering path that replaces jitter and push-ins with clean cuts, crossfades, or short clip reveals.
